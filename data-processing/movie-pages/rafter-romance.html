RAFTER ROMANCE

U.S., 1932
Directed by William A. Seiter
Starring Ginger Rogers, George Sidney, Norman Foster
Approx. 72 min. 35mm. 

Their rent late again, artist/night watchman Norman Foster (then Mr. Claudette Colbert, and later director of the Wellesian JOURNEY INTO FEAR) and telemarketer [sic] Ginger Rogers get an ultimatum: timeshare the attic or out. But as the war of notes on the fridge escalates between the strangers, guess who meets cute outside the building, even as each suffers from admirer overload?

35mm print courtesy Turner Classic Movies and the Library of Congress.
